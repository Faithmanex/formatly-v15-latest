## Typography

- **Font:** Inter (system fallback: ui-sans-serif, system-ui, sans-serif)
- **Headings:** 700 weight
- **Body:** 400 weight
- **Small/Labels:** 400 weight, 0.875rem

## Spacing & Radius

- **Border radius:** `0.5rem` (8px)
- **Card padding:** `1rem–1.5rem` (16–24px)
- **Section padding:** `3rem–5rem` (48–80px)
